Supply Chain Analytics Dashboard & Shipment Delay Prediction

Project Overview

This project analyzes a global supply chain dataset containing over 180,000 transactions to uncover business insights related to revenue, profitability, customer behavior, logistics performance, and delivery risks.

The project combines SQL, Python, Machine Learning, and Power BI to build an end-to-end analytics solution capable of identifying operational bottlenecks and predicting shipment delays.

Objectives

* Analyze sales and profitability across markets, countries, and product categories.
* Identify factors contributing to late deliveries.
* Evaluate customer segments and purchasing behavior.
* Build interactive business intelligence dashboards.
* Develop machine learning models to predict shipment delay risk.

Key Business Insights

* Europe generated the highest revenue among all markets.
* Fishing and Cleats were the highest revenue-generating categories.
* Consumer customers contributed the largest share of sales.
* More than half of all orders experienced delivery delays.
* Shipping mode significantly influenced delivery performance.
* Discount-heavy orders showed higher delivery risk.
